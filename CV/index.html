Drone related skills and experience

Hands on skills and experience

  • Broad hands-on experience in making drones, including design, assembly, and troubleshooting.
  • Manufacturing custom fiber/resin-composite parts in variety of shapes and sizes such as propellers, frames/spars, wings, swashplates, and other mechanical components.
  • Machining aluminium parts such as motor bells using a lathe and a 3-axis CNC mill.
  • FDM 3D printing, including more demanding cases such as printing non fiber filled nylon on an open-bed slinger or optimizing wall extrusion paths for thin-walled parts.
  • General drone assembly work including soldering, static balancing, and dynamic balancing, rewinding motors etc.

Software Skills

  • CAD modelling (FreeCAD).
  • 3d printing slicers (Orca Slicer and PrusaSlicer).

  • CNC workflow: FreeCAD CAM and LinuxCNC. (Basics)
  • Betaflight and INAV, including for example custom mixers and tuning, use of blackbox explorer etc.
  • ArduPilot basics; I have tested basic usage but do not yet have experience with advanced autonomous flight modes.
  • General programming skills in following languages in order of how much I've used them in recent years:

    • C++
